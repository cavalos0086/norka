STRAND: Redisigning

This e-commerce retailer wants to redesign their site

STRAND bookstore that has over 18 miles of new, used and rare books. This small retailer have to make some changes to their site to be successfully competitive with large retailers like Amazon. In this project I designed a clickable prototype that makes the experience of buying online more pleasurable. My role was to research, design and test all UIs related to the project
