COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS

While we waited for our survey to be taken, we conducted research on the top 3 most relevant competitors. Also, we analyzed the user-flows of the most used comparative mobile applications. Our client suggested us to have these threee competitors to our analysis.

COMPARATIVE ANALYSIS

During the survey we found that most people are familiar with Venmo and prefer its flow to do monetary transactions. Therefore, we decided to look a deeper look into Venmo app.

USER INTERVIEWS

After two days of publishing the screener survey, we had 38 total participants. WE contacted 11 people to interview them as a next step. The purpose of user interviews was to uncover pain points, pleasures, needs and motivations about the process of donating to charities.

User Interviews Findings:

Majority:
  • Comfortable with mobile transactions
  • Credit card saved on websites and mobile
  • Discover causes to support on socual media, but may be hesistant to share donation.
  • Frustration with lengthy donation process and may abandon donation
  • Prefer e-mail receipts, easier to reference in the future.

EMPATHY MAPPING

After distilling the interviews we created an empathy map with the insights and quotes from the user interviews. This method helped us to bridge the gap between users and design concepts. Working with the project manager we used the mapping to proceed to persona creation.

We assigned an specific color for each interviewed gor a general understanding, or for specific tasks and situations.

PERSONA DEVELOPMENT

After analyzing the empathy mapping we created 3 personas by grouping common behaviors, to identify users and summarize user research. The personas helped us to keep in mind the goals of the business, solve their problems/needs and test the future ideas of the design

FEATURE PRIORITIZATION

After creating our personas we moved on to feature prioritization. We started by organizing them based on their value and urgency. For this stage of the research, we used two different methods: 2X2 quadrant and value proposition. Working hand by hand with our personas, we were able to determine which features needs to be included in the current design and what could be addressed in further versions.

2x2 Quadrant:

Identied the urgency and importance of every feature
