I did this project while I was in UX school. This project helped me to understand how important is the research part and how our job as a designer is always think that we are not the user. Since I did no research I couldn't find out some dificulties or challenges that the users might have using this website.

The most challenging part of this project was when I worked on some methods that were completely new to me, had to do some research and teach myself the process of them and got the results I was expecting. This challenges helped me to learn new things and learned from my mistakes.
